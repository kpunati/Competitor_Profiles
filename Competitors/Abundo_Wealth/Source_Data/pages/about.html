Our Story

We’re a passionate group of people working to build the future of Financial Planning.

Our team believes the financial services industry is ready for a change. We want to serve everyone, not just those who have already accumulated wealth. We believe in empowering people to have confidence and clarity in their financial decisions. Through our collective expertise and passion, we are creating new industry standards, ensuring a more accessible and informed financial future for everyone.

Our Journey to Abundo

While working as a financial advisor at a fortune 500 company for over a decade, Abundo's founder, Eric Simonson, saw a need to help people who don't have access to work with a traditional advisor. Since many advisors require clients to have a minimum amount of money to work with them, a lot of people can't get the help they need.

Eric left his job and launched a different model: one where he was only paid for giving honest advice that benefited his clients, not himself. He built Abundo around eliminating conflicts of interest and not overcharging for professional advice. He found he was able to help more people using proven low-cost investments and provide guidance on all areas of their financial lives.

This is why Eric created Abundo Wealth.

Your Financial Partner

By decoupling fees from our clients' net worth and being fully transparent with how we're paid, we serve as a true friend in the industry. Our financial planners put YOU in the driver's seat and we're here as a knowledgeable friend to guide you along the way. Since launching in 2019, our business has grown exponentially. We've expanded our team and have been recognized in dozens of interviews, news articles, and podcasts, all because we're finally giving people what they really want:

A financial advisor who puts you first.
