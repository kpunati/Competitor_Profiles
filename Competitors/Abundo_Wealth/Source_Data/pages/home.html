Did You Know...

Many financial advisors work inside a system that incentivizes them to put your needs second - even if they are "fiduciaries" or "fee-only".

Financial service companies can limit their advice to what makes them money, such as high-commission insurance products or annuities, and may not help you with the important guidance you need.
Many financial advisors will only work with you when you have a minimum amount to invest. They also require you to move your accounts to a place where they can manage them, and bill you each month for management fees.
Over time, even a small management fee eats away at your life savings, generating lower investment returns in the long run.
