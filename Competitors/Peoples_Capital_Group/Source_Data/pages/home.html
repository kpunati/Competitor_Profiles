    Frequently Asked Questions

    How much do I need to get started?

    Peoples Capital Group requires a minimum investment of $30,000 to get started.

    Is real estate an historically safe investment?

    Yes, real estate appreciates over time, meaning that even with annual fluctuations, historically your money will grow.

    Can anyone invest with PCG?

    Any individual who meets the 30,000 minimum and has goals that are aligned with Peoples Capital Group will be considered to join our investor community.

    How long will my money be tied up?

    Peoples Capital Group will invest your capital for 3-5 years. At the end of that period, you can choose to either take your profit and exit the investment or you can choose to reinvest again.
