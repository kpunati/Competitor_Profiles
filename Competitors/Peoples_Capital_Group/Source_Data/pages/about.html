The Business Model

The core business operations create passive income streams for people by investing in undervalued NJ apartment buildings with access to NYC. The properties are evaluated and cherry-picked through a proprietary method of advanced marketing and financial techniques and strong relationships within the real estate community.

Investments are syndicated. A group of people invests as an LLC member, and PCG assumes the management and mortgage risk with the bank.

​​Dozens of properties get evaluated each month to assess long-term profitability, risk and optimal returns for their clients. PCG invests thousands of dollars and almost innumerable hours to find attractive real estate opportunities, which it shares with its clients.

Business Acumen

PCG’s reputation for sharp and aggressive business decisions keeps its Investor Community committed and re-investing. Every real estate opportunity has an exit strategy of 3-5 years, and every deal has a targeted return of at least 10%. Historically, 9 out of 10 PCG investors re-invest their capital at the end of each investment.

​PCG only manages PCG properties. Seth and Aaron are hyper-vigilant and laser-focused on managed risk and profit for their clients. They seek to understand each of their clients goals, needs and abilities and to provide the best real estate investment for each individual.  The company has a core group of 50+ investors and has decided to expand its network to help more people invest in real estate.

Business Reach

PCG maintains an active network of 10K-plus, including both real estate and non real estate professionals and other individuals who are interested in what real estate can afford them. The company aims to help as many people as possible within the limited real estate opportunities it has available.
