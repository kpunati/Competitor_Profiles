## Other Initiatives

Passionate about both their work and the collaborative spirit, our team forms a tightly-knit unit that places a high value on integrity and prioritizes the well-being of our clients. At PWL, we embrace our inquisitive nature, celebrating the development opportunities that arise.

## How we take care of our people

At PWL, our people are our priority. It is our goal to support our people’s successes in every way possible. We have designed different program offerings that prioritize our employees and their wellbeing. We provide support through many aspects of wellbeing from physical, emotional, career, and financial.

Some of the benefits offered at PWL include:

-   Work/life balance
-   Minimum 3 weeks vacation
-   Retirement savings matching plan
-   Remote work opportunities available
-   Annual Education Allowance
-   Public Transportation Budget
-   24/7 virtual medical support access
-   Group Benefit Coverage
-   Complimentary Portfolio Management and Financial Planning
-   Corporate Fitness Programs
