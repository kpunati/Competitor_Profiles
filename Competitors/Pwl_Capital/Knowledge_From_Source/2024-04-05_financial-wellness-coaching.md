What are PWL Capital's fiduciary duties?

PWL Capital has a fiduciary duty to its clients, which means we have a legal responsibility to always act in your best interests. We take that responsibility very seriously.

We focus on understanding our clients’ unique circumstances and aspirations and offer the most appropriate solutions at all times.

Who does PWL Capital work with?

We work with clients all over Canada.

PWL Capital is honored to work with well over a thousand Canadian families coast to coast. Whether you’re a entrepreneur, business leader, medical professional, close to/are in retirement, or anything in between, our advisors can help you fund the life you want.

What is PWL Capital Wealth Management Philosophy?

We assist clients in finding and funding a good life. Our portfolio management philosophy relies on academic research that demonstrates that markets are efficient and that prices reflect all available information.

The best way to capture what the markets offer is to have a low-cost, broadly diversified portfolio that matches your risk tolerance.

Is PWL Capital an independent firm?

Yes! PWL Capital was founded in 1996 as an independent firm and has remained so ever since. This independence means that we can serve you without outside influence or pressure.

Our employees are our shareholders. Together, we developed a hundred-year ownership vision that ensures we will remain fiercely independent and continue to provide conflict-free financial advice and portfolio management services to our clients.

What are “Investable Assets”?

The term ‘Investable Assets’ includes all liquid and near-liquid assets, including brokerage accounts, retirement accounts, RRSPs, TFSAs, trusts, etc.

It does not include the value of your home, properties, equity in a business, etc. They represent assets we can invest in on your behalf.

What is the minimum amount of investable assets needed to work with PWL?

While we don’t maintain a strict minimum investment requirement, we carefully select clients who align with our approach and values. We typically work with individuals and families who either have accumulated significant assets or demonstrate strong potential for wealth creation. Our most successful client relationships are with those who value comprehensive financial planning, embrace an evidence-based investment philosophy, and seek a long-term partnership. We evaluate each potential relationship individually to ensure we can provide meaningful value and the high level of service our clients expect.

Do you offer one-time fee-only planning?

No, we do not offer one-time financial planning at PWL Capital. At PWL, we combine ongoing financial planning, implementation, and portfolio management into one comprehensive service offering for our clients. We believe that financial planning is not just a one-time exercise, but rather a dynamic process that requires periodic updates and collaboration between the advisor and client. As your circumstances, goals, and priorities shift, this should be captured in your financial roadmap. Life changes and so should your plan!
