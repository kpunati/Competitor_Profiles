In this episode of Advisors Unscripted, we talk with Colin O. about how money has turned out not to be the prime directive of his work and life. He has committed to running a 1% fee-based financial advisory, electing not charge a percentage on his assets under management. Colin also discusses how his Tik Tok channel gained hundreds of thousands of views. Advisors Unscripted is a weekly podcast that takes a closer look at why financial advisors do what they do, and how that makes them who they are.
