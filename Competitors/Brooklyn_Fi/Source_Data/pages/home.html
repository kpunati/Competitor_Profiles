  • Seeking Answers and Validation

  • Looming Tax Worries and Complications
    You’re doing well financially, but you’re afraid of a huge tax bill and don’t know what to do about it.
  • Finger Pointing and Juggling
    Playing phone tag between your accountant, tax preparer, and financial advisor is exhausting—all while hoping nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Overwhelmed by Equity Decisions
    You feel overwhelmed by stock options, RSUs, and complex equity decisions while HR says, "Ask your tax advisor."

    Working with advisors who don't understand your world, rarely reach out to you, or talk down to you about money makes you feel frustrated. You’re crossing your fingers that someone is proactively watching your investments and thinking about tax implications.

With BKFi

  • One Team, Many Solutions
    One coordinated team handling everything: tax prep, investing, financial planning, and equity decisions that talk to each other so you don't have to.
  • Equity Expertise Meets Strategy
    A tech-savvy partner who gets your equity compensation and can tell you when to trade your employee stock (and even trade for you) that optimizes both your taxes and your long-term wealth.
  • Clear Advice from Real People
    People who can explain complex financial concepts clearly and actually want you to understand what's happening with your money. Regular, proactive updates on your portfolio and market changes in plain English.
  • Quick Answers When You Need Them
    A team you can contact when you need a quick answer about exercising options or buying a house. Plus, 24/7 access to your financial dashboard to check your progress toward goals.
  • Expert Bench and Trusted Network
    A deep bench of CFP® s, CPAs, and ECAs who eat, sleep, and breathe this stuff. Plus, a Rolodex of vetted experts for anything we don't handle directly.
