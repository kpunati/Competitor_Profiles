# PSA Thursday: Year-End Tax Moves to Finish 2020

![](https://affordanything.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/psa-thursday-tax-moves-300x200.jpg)Welcome back to PSA Thursday, a weekly-*ish* segment in which we talk about how to handle money, work, and life in the year that is 2020.

Today, we focus on the importance of year-end tax planning. Make these moves before ringing in 2021!

Here are the tips we cover:

1.  Open a retirement account (make contributions in 2021 that apply to 2020)
2.  Adjust your tax withholdings
3.  Check your 529 Plan (do your contributions qualify for a state income tax deduction or credit?)
4.  Make charitable contributions
5.  Why this could be a good year to make a Roth conversion
6.  Spend down the balance in your FSA (you can only roll over up to $500)

Details about each of these tips are in the episode.

Enjoy, and Happy Holidays!
