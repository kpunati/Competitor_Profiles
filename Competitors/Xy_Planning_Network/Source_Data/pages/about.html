Our integrated support system offers everything independent, fee-only financial planners need to launch, run, and scale the financial planning firm of their dreams with complete autonomy

Real Financial Planning

Created by financial planners for financial planners, XYPN helps make it possible to profitably provide fee-only fiduciary advice in the client's best interest

Real Independence

The only turnkey support platform where its members control 100% of their clients, business, revenue and data AND they choose the level of support they need to succeed throughout their firm journey

Real Support

Our end-to-end ecosystem of technology and support services, resources, and connection with like-minded peers enables successful entrepreneurship for independent financial planners
