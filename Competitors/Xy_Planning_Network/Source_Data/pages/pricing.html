No, you do not! XYPN believes in the CFP® designation and views it as a certification that all financial planners should obtain. However, the only member benefit that requires CFP® certification is the ability to be listed on our Find-An-Advisor profile.

XYPN members agree to the following standards to qualify for membership:

Fee-Only: Members must operate on a fee-only basis. We follow the CFP Board’s definition of Fee-Only: “A certificant may describe his or her practice as “fee-only” if, and only if, all of the certificant’s compensation from all of his or her client work comes exclusively from the clients in the form of fixed, flat, hourly, percentage or performance-based fees.”

Provide Services Virtually As Needed: Members must be able to accommodate clients with mobility/accessibility considerations. Physical location should not limit one’s access to financial planning.

Fee-for-Service (Retainer, Subscription, Hourly, Flat-Fee offering): XYPN members must offer financial planning services to clients without requiring a certain asset level as part of their fee model. Note: Advisors may offset planning fees with AUM.

Good standing with BrokerCheck: Each member is screened via BrokerCheck for approval into the Network. No member is approved if they have a regulatory fine, arbitration or settlement in excess of $10k. Please review this article for more information.

Note: The CFP® designation is required to be listed in the Find-an-Advisor search tool

  • Must not hold out as fee-only at any point, including during the 12-month period, while still receiving trails (and also acknowledges no member can be listed on the XYPN website while receiving any trails)
