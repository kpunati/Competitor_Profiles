# How Much House Can You Afford?

This is how much house you can afford without going broke!

![](https://moneyguy.com/wp-content/themes/moneyguy/img/tool-icon-house.png)

If you can afford more house than you thought, congratulations! Remember, this isn’t the amount you need to spend, and you can always spend less.
