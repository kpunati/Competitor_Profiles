# Are You a Prodigious Accumulator of Wealth?

## Find out if you’re a Prodigious Accumulator of Wealth.

Is your net worth on track for retirement? You might be saving consistently and contributing to your 401(k), but are you actually accumulating wealth at an above-average rate? A Prodigious Accumulator of Wealth (PAW) has twice or more than the expected level of net worth for their age and income, according to the Money Guy-adjusted formula. In this free PDF download, we give the amount you need to have already accumulated by age and income to be considered a PAW.
