# Inside the Life of a Money Guy Millionaire

## What Does It Actually Take to Become a Millionaire?

What do you think your life would look like if you were wealthy? Would you quit your job and travel the world? Would you drive a nice car or eat at Michelin star restaurants?

Think about how you got your fortune. You must have won the Powerball, right? Or maybe it was an inheritance from a rich uncle you never knew. Do you think you’d be happy? That all your worries would just disappear?

We have all of these conceptions of wealth: what it looks like, how to get there, what we’d feel like if we had it. More often than not though, we’re wrong. Horribly wrong.
