# The Money Guy Guide to Retirement

## What would it take to plan retirement with confidence?

Planning for retirement can be overwhelming. Will you have enough money? When can you actually retire? How do you transition from accumulating wealth to spending it? These questions keep many people working longer than necessary or, worse, retiring without a real plan and running out of money.

Retirement isn’t just about the numbers; it’s about designing a fulfilling life that brings purpose, joy, and peace of mind for decades to come. Whether you are 30 or 5 years away from retirement, these nine insights can give you a clear view of the most important factors when planning your great big beautiful tomorrow.

**Common Retirement Planning Questions We Answer:**

-   How do I know if I’m ready to retire?
-   How do I prepare for retirement?
-   Does The Abundance Cycle stop at retirement?
-   How do I make my retirement savings last?

**What’s Inside The Money Guy Guide to Retirement:**

This simple 1-page guide provides 9 insights to help you prepare for and thrive in retirement:

-   **The attitude foundation** – Start with gratitude and appreciation for the life you’ve built
-   **Purpose over escape** – Why knowing what you’re retiring TO matters more than what you’re leaving behind
-   **Do the math**– Stress-test your plan, understand withdrawal rates, and know how to empty your tax buckets strategically
-   **Psychological preparation** – Navigate the mental and emotional transition that catches most retirees off guard
-   **Plus 5 more retirement insights** – Including the importance of lifelong learning, knowing your “why,” and remembering that retirement is supposed to be fun
