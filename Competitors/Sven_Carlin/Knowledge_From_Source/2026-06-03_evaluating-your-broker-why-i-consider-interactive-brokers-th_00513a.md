## The Advantage of Self-Clearing

A significant point of frustration I have encountered with other brokers—such as DEGIRO—is their reliance on third-party clearing entities, such as J.P. Morgan or Société Générale. When these brokers are not self-clearing, they are forced to adhere to third-party policies and potential geopolitical sanctions, which can lead to restricted trading access or frozen assets. As a self-clearing broker, Interactive Brokers maintains direct control over execution, bypassing these third-party limitations.
