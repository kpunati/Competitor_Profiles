Is my subscription to ClearValue Investing tax deductible?

Yes, internet-based subscriptions for websites such as Patreon and ClearValue Investing are considered deductible expenses for tax purposes. If you’re a sole proprietor or small business owner and the subscription was purchased for your business, you can deduct the expense on your tax return (example expense categories: ‘subscriptions’ or ‘training and education’). For additional information, please consult your tax advisor.

ClearValue Investing, its subsidiaries, officers, employees, representatives, and independent contractors are not licensed financial advisors.
