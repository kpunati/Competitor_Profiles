# Productivity Checklist​ - CLEARVALUE INVESTING

**How to Use This Checklist**

This checklist is here to help you stay focused and make consistent progress. Start each day by writing down your top priorities— these are the tasks that matter most and must be worked on today.

For the ‘Self-Improvement’ section, limit yourself to just two items. The goal isn’t to overwhelm yourself, it’s to stay consistent and build momentum.

Don’t treat this checklist like a chore. It’s a tool designed to support your growth and help you achieve real results across every part of your life.

I recommend using this checklist every single day. If you miss a day or even a few, don’t get discouraged. Just get back to it. The key is not to stay off track for too long. The longer you use it, the more progress you’ll see.

For the ‘How I Saved Money Today’ section:

If you save $8 by choosing a store-brand item instead of your usual name-brand, immediately transfer that $8 into a separate savings or brokerage account. That way, you’re actually capturing the savings, not just noticing it. Once you’ve made the transfer, check off the “Transferred” box and take pride in watching your balance grow.
