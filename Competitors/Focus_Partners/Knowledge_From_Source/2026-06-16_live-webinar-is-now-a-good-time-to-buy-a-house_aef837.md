# Live Webinar: Is Now a Good Time To Buy a House?

March 27, 2026

![house and keys](https://focus-financial.transforms.svdcdn.com/production/images/people/7.15_FSC_1600x900.png?w=1600&h=900&auto=compress%2Cformat&fit=crop&dm=1719346078&s=d7c0313fa8fef2e0d1abfd763db763cf)

Recent shifts in the housing market have prompted many potential homebuyers to ask an important question: *Is now a good time to buy*? While inflation has moderated from recent highs and mortgage rates have evolved, affordability challenges and housing supply constraints continue to shape today’s homebuying environment. 

* * *

In this webinar, [Chief Planning Officer Jeffrey Levine](https://www.focuspartners.com/people/jeffrey-levine-cpa-pfs-cfp-aif-cws) explores the key factors buyers should consider when deciding whether they are ready to purchase a home in the current market. The session provides perspective on how interest rates and housing supply interact, how these dynamics influence pricing and affordability, and what practical strategies buyers can evaluate as they plan their next steps. 

In this session, we’ll cover: 

-   Mortgage rates from a historical perspective, and what that means for buyers today
-   How inflation, the Federal Reserve, and housing supply influence affordability
-   How different mortgage rates affect monthly payments and long-term costs
-   Ways buyers may be able to obtain more favorable mortgage terms
-   Additional strategies that may help buyers secure the home they want 

This webinar is ideal for prospective homebuyers who want a clearer, more realistic understanding of today’s housing market. Learn how **interest rates, housing supply, and affordability** affect the buying decision.
