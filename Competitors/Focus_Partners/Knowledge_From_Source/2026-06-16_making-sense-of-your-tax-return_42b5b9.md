# Making Sense of Your Tax Return

June 04, 2026

![](https://focus-financial.transforms.svdcdn.com/production/images/people/Achieving-Success-The-Power-of-Goal-Setting-and-Building-a-Budget-Image.jpg?w=1920&h=1080&auto=compress%2Cformat&fit=crop&dm=1730232080&s=0e76571f88f88612a56ee68d717f4b09)

Most people file a tax return every year but were never taught how to read one.

* * *

 In this webinar, Advanced Planning Advisor Dan Johnson breaks down:

-   How a federal income tax return is "built" from source documents, schedules, worksheets, and the final Form 1040.
-   Key numbers on your return—from gross income and adjustments to deductions, credits, and your final tax bill or refund.
-   What marginal verses effective tax rates mean and when each matter.
-   How to stay on track and avoid penalties through withholdings and estimated tax payments.
-   Strategies to use your tax return as a planning tool, spot opportunities, and mitigate risks before they become problems.

Whether you're a seasoned filer or someone who just signs and sends, this session will help you understand what your tax return is really telling you.
