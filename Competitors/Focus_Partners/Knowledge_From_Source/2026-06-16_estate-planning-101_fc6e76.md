# Estate Planning 101

April 28, 2026

![](https://focus-financial.transforms.svdcdn.com/production/images/people/Legacy_Letters_Image_WIDE.webp?w=1000&h=561&auto=compress%2Cformat&fit=crop&dm=1777386364&s=a4a0156b22b484ffc8c01597136fc74d)

Estate planning doesn’t have to be overwhelming. The first step is understanding your goals—protecting your family, simplifying the future, and ensuring your wishes are honored. A thoughtful plan can bring clarity, reduce stress, and create a lasting legacy. 

* * *

In this session, the Focus Trust and Estate Solutions team shares why estate planning matters, key components of an estate plan, and when to consider a trust.

Learn how a well-designed estate plan can help you:

-   Protect your loved ones by ensuring your estate assets pass smoothly and according to your wishes.
-   Minimize taxes and administrative costs.
-   Avoid probate, which can be a lengthy process and create privacy concerns.
-   Provide for minor children or family members with special needs.
-   Maintain control over your financial and health care decisions if you become unable to act for yourself.
