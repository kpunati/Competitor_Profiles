What does good financial advice look like?

Avise makes it easy for good advisors to thrive, but it isn’t easy to qualify for our platform. We only allow highly qualified planners who have made it through our rigorous screening process.

  • All advisors with Avise are fiduciary, fee-only, Certified Financial Planners®. This is the golden standard of training for our profession. They'll never try to sell you a product and focus solely on your long-term success.

  • Each financial advisor owns their own business. As a co-op, Avise doesn’t employ any of the advisors we match you with. This means you know exactly where your money is going. It also means they aren't bound to any one way of doing things and can freely recommend what's truly best for you.

  • Working with a planning-focused advisor means you'll have a personal relationship. They'll learn what's important to you and help you navigate your complex financial questions. It's an ongoing process, not a one-and-done.

  • Our industry hasn't done a great job opening the door to all kinds of people. We make it easier for those from different backgrounds to get ahead. Diversity breeds unique perspectives and fosters a better experience for you and your money.

  • Our services to your advisor are paid for directly by them, not through your portfolio like other platforms. The collective structure of our co-op makes it possible for us to do more together. You get higher quality advice you can trust is in your best interest.
